# UK Government (Reference Namespace)

Official publications and reports from the United Kingdom government related to AI safety and security.

## Why UK Government References Matter

The UK has positioned itself as a leader in AI safety governance, hosting the AI Safety Summit at Bletchley Park and commissioning the International AI Safety Report.
