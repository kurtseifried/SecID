## CCPA/CPRA Rights

- Right to know
- Right to delete
- Right to opt-out of sale
- Right to non-discrimination
- Right to correct (CPRA)
- Right to limit sensitive data use (CPRA)

## Notes

- CPRA amended and expanded CCPA
- Often sets US privacy standard
- California Privacy Protection Agency (CPPA) enforces
