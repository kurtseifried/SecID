## DFS 500 Requirements

- Cybersecurity program
- CISO designation
- Penetration testing
- Risk assessments
- Incident response
- Third-party oversight

## Notes

- DFS 500 applies to financial services
- SHIELD Act has broad applicability
- Strong enforcement through DFS
