The MIT AI Risk Repository is uniquely comprehensive:

- **74 frameworks synthesized** - Aggregates existing work
- **Dual taxonomies** - Causal and domain-based classification
- **Incident linkage** - Connects risks to real-world incidents
- **Academic rigor** - Peer-reviewed research
